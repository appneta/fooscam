THEN WHO WAS FOOS!?

Welcome to FoosCam!

This thing is still a work in progress but it's more or less working at the moment. Stats are being tracked and triggering on game start and game end events is pretty well functional. If you do, or do not want your stats tracked here's a couple things to keep in mind about how FoosCam watches games.

  • When no ID cards are on the table, no stats are tracked.
  • When four ID cards are on the table (including Guest cards) tracking is activated.
  • While tracking if all four cards cannot be seen by the camera the game is considered over.
  • While tracking if the score for either side goes from 8 or more back to 0 the game is considered over.
  • While tracking if player ID's move from one position to another the game is considered over. This means swapping places with your teammate ends the game.
  • When one of these game over events occurs the team with the highest score on the board before the event is considered the winner.
  • If you're tied 8-8 or 9-9 and a game over event occurs the game goes down as a tie, so take a moment to set the winner to 10 or the loser to 0.
